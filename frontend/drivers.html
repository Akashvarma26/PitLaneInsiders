F1 Drivers

Meet the drivers that make Formula 1 exhilarating!

Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton, born in 1985, is a British legend and a dominant force in Formula One history. Racing for Mercedes, he holds the record for most wins (104) and pole positions (104), and shares the record for most World Championship titles (7) with Michael Schumacher. Hamilton's career is decorated with achievements – numerous podium finishes, hat tricks (winning pole, race, and fastest lap), and even grand slams (winning everything plus leading every lap). Despite not winning a race in 2022, his consistency and skill continue to challenge for the top positions.

Max Verstappen

Max Verstappen, the Dutch daredevil born in 1997, is a three-time World Champion (2021, 2022, 2023) currently racing for Red Bull Racing. Entering F1 at the young age of 17, he shattered records becoming the youngest Grand Prix winner. Verstappen is known for his aggressive driving style and exceptional racecraft. His trophy cabinet boasts an impressive 61 victories (including a record-breaking 19 wins in 2023), 40 pole positions, and 32 fastest laps. He's also achieved multiple hat tricks and grand slams, showcasing his complete dominance on race day. Max Verstappen remains a force to be reckoned with in Formula One.

Charles Leclerc

Monegasque racing driver Charles Leclerc, born in 1997, is a star for Scuderia Ferrari. After dominating GP3 and Formula 2, he entered F1 in 2018. Leclerc boasts impressive achievements, including multiple pole positions (including a championship win in 2019 and 2022), race victories (including his home race of Monaco in 2024), and podium finishes. He even secured a Grand Slam at the 2022 Australian Grand Prix. Currently, Leclerc is a strong contender for the World Championship title.
