F1 Teams


Scuderia Ferrari

Scuderia Ferrari, one of the most illustrious teams in Formula 1. Led by the Team Principal, Mattia Binotto, the team's Technical Director spearheads the design and development of its cars, emphasizing engineering excellence. Notably, the drivers, including Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz Jr., stand as the public face of Ferrari, tasked with maneuvering the scarlet machines to victory.

Visit Ferrari
Mercedes

The Mercedes Formula 1 team, led by the formidable Toto Wolff as team principal, dominates the grid with their cutting-edge technology and strategic prowess. Spearheading their charge is the unparalleled talent of Lewis Hamilton, a seven-time World Champion whose skill and determination consistently propel Mercedes to the front of the pack.

Visit Mercedes

Aston Martin

The Aston Martin F1 Team, based in Silverstone, England, is owned by Lawrence Stroll and features a driver lineup with experience and youth. Two-time Formula One World Champion Fernando Alonso partners with Lance Stroll, the son of the team owner.

Visit Aston Martin

Alpine

The Alpine F1 Team, based in Enstone, Oxfordshire, England, is the rebranded team of Renault, emphasizing French automotive excellence. Under the leadership of Laurent Rossi, the team is aiming for new heights in Formula 1. The driver lineup includes the experienced Pierre Gasly and the talented Esteban Ocon.

Visit Alpine
Kick Sauber

Steeped in history, Stake F1 Team Kick Sauber, formerly Sauber, is a Swiss team on the rise. Backed by title sponsor Stake and with Audi joining the ranks in 2026, they're building a powerful future. The driver line-up boasts experience with veteran Valtteri Bottas and fresh talent with Zhou Guanyu, China's first F1 racer.

Visit Kick Sauber
Haas

The Haas F1 Team, based in Kannapolis, North Carolina, USA, is the first American-led Formula One team in over 30 years. Founded by industrialist Gene Haas, the team made its debut in the 2016 FIA Formula One World Championship. The driver lineup features experienced racer Kevin Magnussen and young talent Nico Hülkenberg.

Visit Haas
